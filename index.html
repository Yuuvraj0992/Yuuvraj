About

A bit about me and what I bring to the cutting room.

Y

Hey, I'm Yuuvraj — a video editor obsessed with pacing, rhythm, and the small choices that make a cut feel alive.

I've worked with creators, agencies, and indie filmmakers to shape footage into stories that land. Whether it's a 15-second hook or a 15-minute doc, I care about the same thing: keeping viewers leaning in.

When I'm not in DaVinci Resolve, you'll find me chasing color references, building sound libraries, and over-analyzing trailers frame by frame. I also lean on Claude to speed up the slow parts — scripting ideas, naming cuts, brainstorming structure — so more of my time goes into the craft.
